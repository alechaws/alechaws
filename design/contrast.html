Contrast

The design principle of contrast deals with visual differences. Thses differences can be found in the colors that are used, the size of different texts, and preactically any other item within a page. Contrast could be white text on a black background, or it could be a single colored item in an otherwise entirely monochromatic page. These differences can be used to draw someone's attention to something.

One website that uses contrast effectively is Ferrari.com. The website of the Itallian automaker uses contrast to draw the viewers eyes to their prooducts. The main page is a black background and then there are elements on the page that use bright and vibrant colors. These elementsbring you through their site to look at the new things that the company is doind, to show you the story of the company's history, and (of course) to draw youy into the product pages fore their latest automotive marvels. Ferrari used the cntrasting colors to draw you in, in hopes that the cars would get you to stay.
