Repetition

Repetition is a design concept that defines itself. It is using the same visual elements throughout a website, as well as using similar elements within different parts of a page.

IFIXIT.com uses repetition to help user's navigate the various pages of their site. Ifixit has many aspects to their website. They have how-to guides for DIY electronics repair, a store with parts and tools, a news feed with news relating to gadget repair and technology, adn several more pages on top of those. All of the pages have a similar layout, with tiles that help users to navigate the various sections of the site. The tile motif is repeated on every page within the site.
