Full-Time Missionary Housing

Temple View Inn & Suites is proud to offer long-term housing solutions for Full-Time temple missionaries. Our suites come equipped with a king-sized bed, a full kitchen, and on-site laundry facilities.

We also offer special amenities such as a shuttle that provides transportation to the temple in coordination with normal shift rotations for the missionaries.

Please contact our missionary coordinators for assistance in answering questions and with reserving a suite for your mission.
