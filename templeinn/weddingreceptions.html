Temple View Inn & Suites Reception Hall

At Temple View Inn & Suites, we know that it can be difficult to find a venue for a wedding reception that is in a convenient location. This is why we have a full-service reception hall located at each one of our hotels. We offer venue services, catering services, and setup services as well. Each reception can be tailored to meet your exact wants and needs.

Venue Services

Each of our hotels has a spacious reception hall that is large enough to house a gathering of any size. Reservations can be made as much as one year in advance. To call to make a reservation or to get a quote, call our event planners at (208) 867-5309

Each of our reception halls has a fully stocked kitched that can either me staffed by our kitchen crew, or can be rented alongside the reception hall if you prefer to prepare your own dishes. Ask the event planners for more details.

Our event staff can handle as much or as little of the background work as you would like. They can handle all of the setup and takedown work while you man the kitchen, they can take care of everything, or they can just be there to help clean up at the end of the night.
